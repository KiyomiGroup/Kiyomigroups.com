FAQs

Questions we get asked a lot

How long does a typical project take?↓
It depends on the scope. A simple website takes 2 to 4 weeks. A full web app or mobile app is typically 8 to 16 weeks. We will give you a detailed timeline after our discovery call. We never give estimates before we understand the project properly.
How much does it cost?↓
Every project is quoted individually based on scope, complexity, and timeline. We are transparent about pricing from day one and will never inflate an invoice after the fact. Use the contact form to share your project details and we will come back with a clear, itemised estimate.
Who owns the code when you are done?↓
You own 100% of everything we build. All source code, design files, assets, and infrastructure are transferred to you upon final payment. There is no vendor lock in with Kiyomi Group.
Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?↓
Yes. We work with clients across Africa, the UK, the US, and beyond. All project communication happens over video call, Slack, and Notion. Time zone differences have never been a problem for us.
Can you work with my existing team?↓
Absolutely. We regularly embed our engineers and designers into existing teams. We use your tools, follow your processes, and integrate as smoothly as possible. Many clients use us to extend their in house capacity for specific projects or timelines.
