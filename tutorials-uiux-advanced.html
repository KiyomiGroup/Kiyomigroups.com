Curriculum

Detailed, practical, project-led.

Design Strategy & Product Thinking

Week 1–2

Move beyond execution into strategy. Learn how to define design vision, run design sprints, map opportunity areas, and present design as a business driver — not just a deliverable.

Advanced Accessibility & Inclusive Design

Week 3

WCAG 2.2 compliance, ARIA patterns, colour contrast, keyboard navigation, screen reader UX, and how to audit existing products for accessibility gaps. Build inclusion into every design decision.

Designing with Data — Analytics & A/B Testing

Week 4

Read and act on product analytics using tools like Mixpanel and Amplitude. Design and interpret A/B tests. Learn how to use quantitative data alongside qualitative research.

Complex Interaction Design & Motion

Week 5

Advanced prototyping with Figma, Framer, and ProtoPie. Design meaningful micro-interactions and transition animations that improve usability — not just aesthetics.

Design Ops & Team Infrastructure

Week 6

Set up design processes for growing teams: critique rituals, documentation standards, onboarding for new designers, tooling decisions, and how to run design at scale without quality slipping.

AI for Advanced Designers

Week 7

Use generative AI for pattern research, automated documentation, and rapid iteration. Understand AI product design patterns — designing AI features that users can actually trust and control.

Leadership, Mentoring & Career Growth

Week 8–9

Lead design critiques, mentor junior designers, negotiate for design's seat at the table, and build your personal brand as a senior design professional.

Capstone: Full Product Design Audit

Week 10

Conduct a complete UX audit of a real product: research, heuristic evaluation, competitive analysis, and a redesign proposal. This is your senior-level portfolio piece.
