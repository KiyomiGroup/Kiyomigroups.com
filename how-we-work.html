How We Work

Transparent. Structured. No surprises.

Most agencies are a black box. You send them money, you wait, you hope. We are the opposite. Here is exactly what happens when you work with us, week by week, from first call to launch day and beyond.

1
Week 1 to 2

Discovery and Planning

This is where we get to know each other and your project. We run structured discovery sessions to deeply understand your users, your business goals, your constraints, and what success actually looks like for you.

What we do in this phase:

  • → Kickoff call to align on vision, goals, and constraints
  • → User research and stakeholder interviews
  • → Technical feasibility assessment
  • → Project scope, milestones, and budget confirmation
  • → Delivery of a detailed project brief
2
Week 2 to 5

Design and Strategy

Before anyone writes code, you will have seen exactly what we are building. We design wireframes, user flows, and full high fidelity screens in Figma and walk you through every decision. You approve before we build.

What you get:

  • → User flow diagrams and information architecture
  • → Low fidelity wireframes for feedback
  • → Full high fidelity UI designs in Figma
  • → Interactive prototype for user testing
  • → Design system and component library
3
Ongoing

Build and Develop

We build in two week sprints with weekly check ins. You always know what we built last week, what we are building this week, and what is coming next. No black box, no radio silence, no surprises on the invoice.

Our development rhythm:

Weekly

Video sync + written progress update

Fortnightly

Sprint demo, your team tests and gives feedback

Always

Slack access, 24hr response, shared Notion board

4
Launch

Testing and Launch

We do not ship and disappear. Before launch we run full QA across all browsers, devices, and use cases. After launch we monitor closely, fix anything that surfaces, and hand over everything you need to run the product yourself.

What launch includes:

  • → Full browser and device QA testing
  • → Performance and accessibility audit
  • → Deployment and infrastructure setup
  • → 30 day post launch bug warranty
  • → Full code handover and documentation
5
Ongoing

Ongoing Support and Growth

Many of our clients stay with us long after launch. We offer retainer based support for maintenance, new feature development, and as a long term technical partner. You can also choose to take the product fully in house and we will make sure that handover is smooth.

Monthly retainer support
Feature sprints
Technical advisory
Team handover and training
Our Principles

The way we think about work

🧠

Understand first, build second

We never start building until we are certain we understand the problem. A week of research saves months of rebuilding.

📣

Radical transparency

If something is behind schedule, we tell you immediately. If we made a mistake, we own it. No cover ups, no spin.

📦

Ship small, ship often

We prefer to get something working in front of real users quickly. Real feedback beats internal assumptions every time.

🔒

It is your product

Every line of code we write is yours. No vendor lock in, no proprietary systems that only we can maintain.

✂️

Simplicity over cleverness

Clever code is hard to maintain. We favour clear, simple solutions that the next developer can understand in five minutes.

📈

We care about your business

We ask about your revenue model, your growth goals, and your users. We build products that help your business succeed.
