10-Week Curriculum

The most complete AI course you'll find.

20+ tools. Real projects. Practical from day one.

Week 1 — Understanding AI: How It Works & What It Can't Do

Week 1

The fundamentals of large language models (LLMs), how AI generates responses, what hallucinations are and why they happen, and how to fact-check AI output. Compare ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini vs Grok — understanding which tool wins for which task.

Week 2 — Prompt Engineering: The Core Skill

Week 2

The difference between a bad prompt and a great one. Frameworks: chain-of-thought, few-shot, role prompting, and context engineering. Control tone, format, length, and depth. Learn to iterate on AI output to get exactly what you need — every time.

Week 3 — ChatGPT Deep Dive: Advanced Features

Week 3

Custom GPTs, Projects, memory, browsing, image generation with DALL-E 3, Advanced Data Analysis (code interpreter), and Sora for video generation. Build your own custom GPT tuned for a specific job — a writing assistant, research tool, or customer support bot.

Week 4 — Claude, Gemini & Perplexity Mastery

Week 4

When and why to use Claude for long documents, nuanced reasoning, and safe content. Gemini for Google Workspace integration and multimodal tasks. Perplexity for citation-backed research and market analysis. Use all three together strategically.

Week 5 — AI Image Generation: Midjourney, Firefly & DALL-E

Week 5

Master Midjourney prompts — styles, parameters, aspect ratios, and modifiers. Adobe Firefly for commercial-safe images. DALL-E 3 for inline generation. Use AI images for marketing, social media, product mockups, and branding — legally and professionally.

Week 6 — AI for Productivity: Microsoft Copilot & Notion AI

Week 6

Microsoft Copilot across Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook — automate reports, summarise emails, generate presentations, and analyse spreadsheets. Notion AI for knowledge management and documentation. Build AI-powered work systems that save hours every week.

Week 7 — AI Automation: Zapier, Make & AI Agents

Week 7

Build no-code AI workflows with Zapier and Make (Integromat). Create automations that post to social media, send emails, update spreadsheets, and process data — all triggered automatically. Introduction to AI agents: autonomous systems that take multi-step actions.

Week 8 — AI for Video & Voice: Sora, ElevenLabs & InVideo

Week 8

Generate AI video with Sora and RunwayML. Clone your voice or create custom AI voices with ElevenLabs and Play.ht. Create full AI-powered YouTube videos, social content, and podcasts. Includes a walkthrough of launching an AI content channel from scratch.

Week 9 — AI Ethics, Safety & Responsible Use

Week 9

Understand the real risks: deepfakes, misinformation, privacy, bias, and IP concerns. How to fact-check AI output, stay on the right side of copyright, and use AI responsibly in professional and creative contexts. Essential knowledge for anyone using AI at work.

Week 10 — Capstone: Build an AI-Powered Product or Business

Week 10

Combine everything you've learned. Build and launch an AI-powered product, content business, or automated workflow. Options include: an AI content agency, a custom GPT business tool, an automated social media system, or an AI-assisted freelance service.
