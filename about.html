Our Story

Started with a simple frustration.
Built into something real.

Kiyomi Group was founded because we kept seeing the same problem: businesses and founders with genuinely good ideas, but no reliable team to help them build those ideas into products.

Too many agencies over-promised and under-delivered. Too many freelancers disappeared mid-project. Too many great ideas ended up as half-finished products collecting dust.

We decided to do it differently. When a client signs with us, we don't just "assign a developer." We sit down, understand the project deeply, and then pull together the exact team it needs — designers, engineers, product thinkers — matched specifically for that work.

That model gives our clients access to senior-level talent without the overhead of hiring a full team. And it means every person working on your project is genuinely the right fit for it.

"We built Kiyomi because we believe every business deserves access to a skilled, honest, reliable tech team — not just the ones with the biggest budgets."
