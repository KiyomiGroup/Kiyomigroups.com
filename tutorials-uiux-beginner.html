6-Week Curriculum

From zero to your first design portfolio.

Week 1 — What is UX & UI Design?

Week 1

The difference between UX and UI, the role of a designer in a product team, and what the end-to-end design process looks like. Introduction to Figma — your main design tool throughout the course.

Week 2 — Understanding Users

Week 2

User research basics: how to interview users, create personas, and map user journeys. Learn to identify pain points and opportunities. Practical exercise: interview 3 real people and synthesize your findings.

Week 3 — Visual Design Fundamentals

Week 3

Typography, colour theory, visual hierarchy, whitespace, and layout. The design principles every great UI is built on. You'll critique real apps and redesign weak UIs to practice what you learn.

Week 4 — Wireframing & Information Architecture

Week 4

Structure before style. Build low-fidelity wireframes in Figma, learn sitemaps and user flows, and practice the double-diamond design thinking methodology used by top product teams globally.

Week 5 — High-Fidelity UI & Prototyping

Week 5

Turn your wireframes into polished, pixel-perfect UI designs. Add colours, images, and micro-interactions. Build a fully clickable, interactive prototype in Figma that you can share with real users.

Week 6 — Usability Testing & Portfolio

Week 6

Test your prototype with real users, document findings, and iterate on feedback. Present your final case study — packaged as a portfolio piece you can immediately use when applying for junior design roles.
