The Kiyomi Playbook

Kiyomi Group is a design-led engineering partner. We bring an open-source mindset to proprietary systems: clear documentation, transparent decisions, and continuous improvement baked into every engagement.

Open-source mindset

We favour techniques from open-source: readable code, small pull requests, and public‑style changelogs so your team can understand how and why the system evolved.

Product & engineering style guides

We leave behind design systems, API standards, and engineering playbooks so your internal teams can ship with the same consistency long after we ship v1.

Continuous improvement

Retrospectives, metrics, and feedback loops are part of our default engagement model— we tune the system and the team, not just the release.
